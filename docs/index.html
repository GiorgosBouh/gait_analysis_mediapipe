Report explanations are generated locally from evidence-based rules and sanity checks. No cloud model or external AI service is required.
This system is a low-cost markerless gait screening and rehabilitation monitoring tool based on monocular video and MediaPipe pose estimation. It provides automated spatiotemporal estimates and heuristic kinematic interpretation for observational use, but it is not a replacement for instrumented clinical gait laboratory analysis.
  • Accuracy: 30-50mm error.
  • Valid for Screening/Research.
  • Use a strict 90° side view with the full body visible from head to feet.
  • Place the camera on a tripod at pelvis-to-hip height and keep the lens level.
  • Keep camera distance, zoom, floor surface, footwear, and lighting stable across sessions.
  • For the static stance: stand upright, look forward, keep both arms relaxed by the sides, feet comfortably apart, feet flat on the floor, knees relaxed but not bent, and remain still for a few seconds.
  • Gait mode: capture the static baseline first, then record at least 3-5 clean gait cycles.
  • Jump mode: start from quiet standing, perform one clear countermovement jump, and land back inside the same side-view capture zone.
  • TUG mode: keep the chair, walking path, and turning point inside the same side-view frame, begin seated and still, then finish clearly seated again.
  • Local Processing.
  • GDPR Compliant.
